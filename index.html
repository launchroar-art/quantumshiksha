About Us

We connect learning, livelihood, and long-term community growth.

Quantum Shiksha Pragati Foundation is registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013. Our work is grounded in a simple idea: access to education and dignified income should go hand in hand.

We train women in crochet and handmade crafts, support educational awareness, and create opportunities for self-reliance in underserved communities.

Our Mission

Empower underprivileged communities through education and sustainable livelihood opportunities.

Our Vision

Build a self-reliant society where opportunity is not limited by income or background.

Our Focus

Women upliftment, crochet skill development, educational support, and community wellbeing.
