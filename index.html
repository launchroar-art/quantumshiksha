About Our Foundation

QuantumShiksha Pragati Foundation is dedicated to providing free and quality education to underprivileged and economically weaker section children, especially those from slums, rural, and tribal areas. We believe education is the foundation for breaking the cycle of poverty and creating opportunities for a better future.

Registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013, our foundation operates across India with a mission to empower communities through education, digital literacy, and skill development programs.

Our Key Initiatives

Quality Education

Establishing schools, learning centers, and libraries focused on basic education, digital education, and life skills for underprivileged children.

Digital Literacy

Providing computer training and digital skills to children and adults, enabling them to become self-reliant in the digital age.

Skill Development

Organizing vocational training programs in tailoring, data entry, digital marketing, and spoken English for employment opportunities.
