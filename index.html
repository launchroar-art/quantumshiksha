About Our Foundation

QuantumShiksha Pragati Foundation is dedicated to providing free and quality education to underprivileged and economically weaker children, especially those from rural and backward regions.

Registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, our foundation works to uplift communities through education, digital literacy, and skill development initiatives.

Our Key Initiatives

Quality Education

Teaching programs, free tuitions, books, and learning support for students.

Digital Literacy

Computer education and digital training classes for children & adults.

Skill Development

Vocational training, spoken English, and employment-oriented programs.
